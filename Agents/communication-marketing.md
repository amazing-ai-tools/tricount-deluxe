# Communication Marketing — Tricount Deluxe

## Positioning
Tricount Deluxe should help groups split expenses clearly, see who owes whom, and settle up without spreadsheet work.

## Audience
- Friends traveling together
- Roommates sharing household costs
- Event organizers collecting and splitting purchases
- Small groups who want simple debt summaries

## Messaging Guidance
- Do not claim features that are not implemented.
- Lead with concrete outcomes: add expenses, split fairly, settle quickly.
- Use examples from trips, meals, shared gifts, and apartments.
- Keep copy direct and low-friction.
